Hampi, Karnataka

Information: Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is renowned for its ancient temples, ruins of palaces, and a historical bazaar. The Virupaksha Temple and Vittala Temple with its musical pillars are major attractions here.

Prices:

- Road: ₹500-₹1,500 by bus; ₹2,000-₹3,000 by car from Bangalore, with a journey time of 6-8 hours. - Rail: ₹300-₹1,000 to Hospet Junction, the nearest station, about 13 km from Hampi. - Air: ₹2,500-₹5,000 to Hubli or Belgaum, then ₹1,500-₹2,500 for a taxi or bus to Hampi, totaling around 6-7 hours travel time.
